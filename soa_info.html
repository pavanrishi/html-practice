Siksha 'o' Anushandhan(soa), formerly Siksha'o'Anushandhan (SOA UNIVERSITY), is a private deemed univesrity located at bhubaneswar,Odisha,India. The universty is composed of nine degree-granting schools and colleges and has a student body of around 15,000.Many of SOA's programs are nationally accredited.
